About Me

Thinker —> Designer —> Builder

My goal is to become successful in thinking about solutions to product design problems, designing experiences that translate user needs into usable, intuitive, and visually appealing interfaces, and helping bring those designs to life.

I'm currently finishing up studying Computer Science and Cognitive Brain Sciences at Tufts University. My work experience has mainly been in front-end engineering and application building, but through these experiences, as well as independent projects, I had autonomy in thinking up, prototyping, and implementing my own design insights. I want to transition from purely a software engineer, to someone who is able to be involved in all parts of the design process.

Aside from software engineering and design I love to cook, travel, and read! (Some favorites — to cook: Shakshouka, country I’ve visited: Iceland, book: The Stranger).
