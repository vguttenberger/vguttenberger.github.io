ABOUT

👋 Hi, I'm Vera! I am a product designer and former front-end developer.
💻 I currently work at Klaviyo, an email and mobile marketing automation platform, with a built-in CDP. I am a senior product designer crafting content editing experiences and interfaces for our customers. Before joining the Editors team (email and mobile message content creation), I worked on our Onboarding team and the SMS Conversations capability.
📊 Previously, I worked at Deloitte's InsightStudio, designing data visualizations and other analytics capabilities for a variety of clients in industries such as biotechnology, consumer products, pharmaceuticals, and government.
🎓 Before that, I graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science and in Cognitive and Brain Sciences from Tufts University.
🎨 I am passionate about creating simple, intutivie, and delightful user experiences. I want to focus on not losing sight of creativity and human connection, which I think is often put on the back burner in a field that is becoming increasingly automated and systematized.
🤝 I also focus a lot on design and development collaboration: in my experience, some of the core issues in product development arise due to barriers in communication between design and development teams. Creating a culture where designers and developers work closely in tandem is a huge step in creating better, user-centered products and services.
🌱 Some areas I'm excited to learn more about and grow in are motion design, typography, and accessible/inclusive design.
✨ In my free time I like to make pottery 🏺, cook new recipes 🥘, read books 📚, watch movies 🎥, and rock climb 🧗
